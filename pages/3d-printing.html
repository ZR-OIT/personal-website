3D Printing

In early 2022, I got my first 3D printer - a Creality Ender 3 V2. I was always fascinated by the idea of being able to model and print just about anything you could think of, so I got a printer to try it for myself. I have been learning how to make 3D models in Autodesk Fusion 360 and how to use Ultimaker Cura for slicing and printing. The 3D models that I design will be available for download here so you can print your own!
